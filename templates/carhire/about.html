Who We Are

MOTRAYLEV Car Services is a trusted car hire company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We offer reliable, safe, and affordable transportation solutions for personal, corporate, and special event needs.

Supported by strategic investor partnerships, our growing fleet ensures that clients always have access to dependable vehicles whenever and wherever they need them.
